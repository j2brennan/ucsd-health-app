UCSD Health Concierge Experience Initiative

System Overview: Health Experience as a Service (HEaaS)


Core Components:

1. Patient Concierge Mobile App

  • Bed status updates
  • Parking/wayfinding assistance
  • Real-time notifications
  • AI assistant for support and FAQs

2. AI Bed and Service Bot

  • LLM-based conversational agent
  • Answers key logistical questions
  • Supports patients and families

3. VIP Premium Packages

  • Paid upgrades (transport, lounges)
  • Wellness services
  • Personalized digital concierge

4. Operational Dashboard

  • Real-time patient and bed data
  • Proactive anomaly detection

5. Post-Discharge Services

  • White-glove recovery assistance
  • Wellness check-ins and telehealth
